Smooth Horizons Video Series

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

To assist in selling the Sam's Club Duraglide 6 Shave System, my team produced a series of in-club and online videos around the concept of men who couldn't afford the high price of razors and met in a Smooth Horizons group counseling session to discuss their issues. Subsequent videos in the series followed attendees as they discovered the Duraglide system and realized they could get a close, comfortable shave at Sam's Club without giving their wallet razor-burn.
